Key Capabilities
🧠
MobileNetV2 Backbone
Fine-tuned on 3,662 labelled fundus images with ImageNet pretrained weights and last-30-layer fine-tuning strategy.
🎯
Focal Loss Optimised
Addresses severe class imbalance in DR datasets using focal loss (γ=2, α=0.25) with class-weight balancing.
📊
5-Grade Classification
Predicts DR severity from Grade 0 (No DR) to Grade 4 (Proliferative) with full probability distribution output.
⚡
Real-Time Inference
Sub-second prediction on CPU. Upload any PNG/JPG/TIFF fundus image and receive results instantly.
💊
Clinical Recommendations
Each grade is paired with grade-specific clinical action guidance, referral urgency, and follow-up timelines.
🔬
Research Grade
Built on the APTOS 2019 Blindness Detection competition dataset. Suitable for academic research and screening studies.
